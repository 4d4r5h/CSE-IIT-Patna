Selection of Micro Problems
---------------------------

Classify as a grievance / Complaint and breakdown
Eg: Cancer is a problem -> Grievance.
	Over a million ppl suffer from oral cancer, need to undergo surgery. -> Statistical Statement
	Oral Cancer surgery develops a lot of side effects. -> 
	80% of ppl who undergo Grade 2/3 oral cancer surgery have chewing problem

Be extremely specific on the problem to solve. Breakdown the broad problem.

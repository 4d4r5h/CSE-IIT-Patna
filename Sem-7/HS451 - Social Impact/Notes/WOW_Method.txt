WOW Method
-----------
A way to solve WHAT for WHOM in order to achieve OUTCOME
Eg: A way to design effective learning environment for classroom Students
  in order to improve their Class performance

Eg: A way to design effective learning environment for classroom Students
  in order to improve their marks.


What: How to design the most effective learning environment
Whom: Students
Metrics: Gap between delivery and comprehension should reduce.
  Marks/Engagement/Class performance
